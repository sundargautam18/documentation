Annapurna Art is an online art marketplace that connects artists with collectors worldwide. Artists sell original paintings, sculptures, photography, and drawings directly to buyers, without needing a physical gallery or an intermediary.

## What Annapurna Art does

At its core, Annapurna Art is a two-sided marketplace:

- **Collectors** can browse and search original artwork by category, such as painting, or by theme, such as artwork from a particular country, and buy directly from the artists who created it.
- **Artists** can list and sell their original work to a global audience, manage their listings, and handle orders through the platform.

Because purchases happen directly between the collector and the artist, the platform is designed to keep the artist close to the sale, from listing the work to fulfilling the order.

## How the platform connects artists and collectors

Annapurna Art aims to make it straightforward for artists to reach a global audience and for collectors to discover original work they wouldn't otherwise have access to. Artists can sign up for free and list their work, and collectors can browse the marketplace by category or theme, view individual artist profiles, and purchase artwork directly.

For a closer look at how the two sides of the marketplace work together, see [Platform Overview](/introduction/platform-overview). For a list of what the platform currently offers, see [Key Features](/introduction/key-features).
