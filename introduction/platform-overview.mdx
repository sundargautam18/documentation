Annapurna Art has two sides: collectors who browse and buy original artwork, and artists who list and sell it. This page walks through how each side experiences the platform and how a typical transaction connects them.

<Warning>
  This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, and labels have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## The two sides of the platform

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Collectors">
    Collectors use Annapurna Art to discover and purchase original artwork directly from artists.

    - Browse or search artwork by category, such as painting, sculpture, photography, or drawing
    - Filter by theme or country, such as artwork from Nepal
    - View individual artist profiles and their available work
    - Save pieces to a wishlist for later
    - Purchase artwork directly from the artist, with shipping paid by the collector

    See the [Buyer Guide](/buyer-guide/browsing-artwork) for detailed walkthroughs.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Artists">
    Artists use Annapurna Art to reach collectors worldwide without needing a physical gallery.

    - Sign up for free, with no fee to join or list work
    - Manage a portfolio and listings from the Supplier Dashboard
    - Add, edit, and update artwork listings, including up to 5 images per artwork
    - Respond to sales and track orders from anywhere in the world
    - Receive payouts via Khalti or credit card

    See the [Artist Guide](/artist-guide/becoming-an-artist) for detailed walkthroughs.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How a transaction connects both sides

At a high level, a sale on Annapurna Art typically moves through these stages:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Artist lists artwork">
    An artist signs up for free, sets up their portfolio, and adds artwork listings through the Supplier Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collector discovers artwork">
    A collector browses or searches the marketplace by category, theme, or country, and views the artist's profile and listing details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collector purchases artwork">
    The collector buys the piece directly from the artist and pays for shipping as part of the purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Artist fulfills the order">
    The artist tracks the order from their Supplier Dashboard and ships the artwork to the collector.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Artist receives payout">
    The artist receives payment through their chosen payout method, either Khalti or credit card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The exact screens, order statuses, and payout timing for this flow haven't been independently verified. See [Orders and Sales](/artist-guide/orders-and-sales) and [Order Tracking](/buyer-guide/order-tracking) for more detail, and confirm specifics against the live site.
</Note>
