Annapurna Art is an online art marketplace where artists sell original paintings, sculptures, photography, and drawings directly to collectors around the world. These docs help you get the most out of the platform, whether you're browsing for your next favorite piece or building your presence as an artist.

<Info>
  New to Annapurna Art? Start with [About Annapurna Art](/introduction/about) for a quick overview of the platform, or jump straight into [Creating an Account](/getting-started/creating-an-account) if you're ready to get started.
</Info>

## Who these docs are for

Annapurna Art serves a few different groups of people, each with their own workflows and goals.
