Annapurna Art lets artists sell original paintings, sculptures, photography, and drawings directly to collectors around the world. Joining as an artist is free, and you can start sharing your portfolio with a global audience of buyers.

<Warning>
Some details on this page — such as exact eligibility requirements and the full artist onboarding experience — have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review). Confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## Why sell on Annapurna Art

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free to join" icon="circle-check">
    Annapurna Art does not charge artists for platform access. Signing up and listing your work costs nothing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Global collector base" icon="earth-americas">
    Your artwork is visible to collectors browsing the marketplace from anywhere in the world, not just your local area.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Supplier Dashboard" icon="gauge">
    Manage your portfolio and respond to sales from anywhere, using a dedicated dashboard built for artists.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Direct sales" icon="handshake">
    Sell directly to collectors without going through a gallery or intermediary.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

Signing up as an artist is designed to be quick and easy. You complete a profile, and once it's set up, you can start sharing your portfolio right away. From there, you use the Supplier Dashboard to add artwork, track orders, and manage your sales.

<Note>
Whether Annapurna Art takes a commission on sales, and the exact registration requirements, have not been confirmed. See [Artwork Pricing](/artist-guide/artwork-pricing) for what's known and what still needs verification.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does it cost anything to sign up as an artist?">
    No. Annapurna Art does not charge artists for platform access — signing up is free.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a professional portfolio to apply?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. Annapurna Art describes signup as quick and easy, with artists able to start sharing their portfolio instantly after completing a profile, which suggests there isn't a lengthy review process — but exact eligibility criteria aren't verified.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I sell any type of art?">
    Annapurna Art supports original paintings, sculptures, photography, and drawings. If you work in another medium, check with [support](/support/contact-support) before signing up.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Annapurna Art take a commission on sales?">
    This is not confirmed either way. See [Artwork Pricing](/artist-guide/artwork-pricing) for details and what still needs to be verified.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
