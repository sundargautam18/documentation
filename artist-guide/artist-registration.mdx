Registering as an artist on Annapurna Art is free and designed to be quick. This guide walks you through the sign-up flow so you can start sharing your portfolio with collectors.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, field names, and rules have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

If you want to buy artwork instead of sell it, see [Creating an Account](/getting-started/creating-an-account) for the collector sign-up path.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the artist sign-up page">
    From the [Annapurna Art homepage](https://annapurnaart.com/), open your account menu and choose the option to sign up as an artist, or visit the [Sell your artwork](https://annapurnaart.com/footer/sell-your-artworks) page directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the artist/seller option">
    If the sign-up flow asks you to choose a role, select the option for selling artwork rather than buying it. This keeps your account on the artist path.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the registration form">
    Enter the details requested, typically your name, email address, and a password. You may also be asked for basic information about yourself as an artist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your artist profile">
    Add profile details that collectors will see, such as an artist bio, profile photo, and location. This is what forms your public artist page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and accept the terms">
    Read the terms of service and any artist-specific seller agreement, then confirm that you agree before submitting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your registration">
    Submit your details to create your artist account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email (if required)">
    Check your inbox for a confirmation message and follow the link or instructions provided to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Once your profile is complete, you can start sharing your portfolio right away — Annapurna Art describes this as instant, without a lengthy waiting period. This hasn't been independently verified, so confirm what happens immediately after you submit your registration.
</Tip>

<Note>
Registration gives you access to the [Artist Dashboard](/artist-guide/artist-dashboard), which is where you manage your portfolio, listings, and orders going forward.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there a fee to register as an artist?">
    No. Annapurna Art does not charge artists for platform access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does registration take?">
    Annapurna Art describes artist sign-up as quick and easy, but the exact time and any review or approval steps haven't been confirmed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I register as both a collector and an artist?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. If you already have a collector account, check your account settings for an option to also register as an artist, or [contact support](/support/contact-support).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens after I submit my registration?">
    You should gain access to the Artist Dashboard, where you can complete your profile and start listing artwork. Exact next steps in the flow haven't been independently verified.
  </Accordion>
