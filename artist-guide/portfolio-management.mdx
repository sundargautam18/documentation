Your portfolio is your public presence on Annapurna Art — it's what collectors see when they visit your artist profile, such as [this example artist page](https://annapurnaart.com/artist/92). Managing it well helps collectors understand your work and decide to buy.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact profile fields, layout, and customization options have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## Managing your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Sign in and go to your [Artist Dashboard](/artist-guide/artist-dashboard).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your profile settings">
    Look for a profile or "About Me" section where you can edit your bio, photo, and other public details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your details">
    Keep your bio, photo, and contact preferences current so collectors get an accurate impression of you and your work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review how your portfolio looks to collectors">
    Visit your public artist page to see your portfolio the way a collector would.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
A clear, well-written bio and a good profile photo can make your artist page more compelling. Consider looking at other [artist profiles](https://annapurnaart.com/artist) for inspiration.
</Tip>

<Note>
Your portfolio is separate from individual listing management. To add, edit, or organize the artwork itself, see [Uploading Artwork](/artist-guide/uploading-artwork), [Managing Artwork](/artist-guide/managing-artwork), and [Updating Artwork](/artist-guide/updating-artwork).
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can collectors see my full portfolio in one place?">
    Yes — Annapurna Art has individual artist profile pages, such as the [artist directory](https://annapurnaart.com/artist), where collectors can view an artist's available work.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I customize the layout of my artist page?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. Check your dashboard's profile settings for available customization options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Should I remove sold pieces from my portfolio?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. Many marketplaces keep sold work visible (marked as sold) to showcase your body of work, but check your dashboard for available options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do collectors find my portfolio?">
    Collectors can browse the artist directory or find your profile linked from your individual artwork listings. See [Browsing Artwork](/buyer-guide/browsing-artwork) for the collector-side view.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
