---

Once you're registered as an artist, you can start listing your work from the Supplier Dashboard. This guide walks you through uploading a new piece of artwork.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact form fields, upload interface, and validation rules have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing. The 5-image limit noted below is confirmed; other details are not.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in and open your dashboard">
    Sign in to your artist account and go to your [Artist Dashboard](/artist-guide/artist-dashboard).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new artwork listing">
    Look for an option to add or upload new artwork from your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload images of your artwork">
    Add photos of your piece. Annapurna Art allows up to 5 images per artwork, so consider including different angles, close-ups of detail, and, where relevant, a shot showing scale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add artwork details">
    Fill in information about the piece, such as its title, medium, dimensions, and a description. Exact required fields haven't been confirmed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a price">
    Enter a price for the artwork. See [Artwork Pricing](/artist-guide/artwork-pricing) for what's known about how pricing works.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and publish">
    Review your listing details and images, then submit to publish the artwork to your portfolio.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/artist-guide/uploading-artwork.png" alt="Artwork upload form showing image slots and listing details" />
</Frame>

<Note>
You can share up to 5 different images per artwork. Using multiple images can help collectors evaluate a piece more confidently before buying.
</Note>

<Tip>
Use clear, well-lit photos. Since collectors can't view the piece in person, your images are the main way they judge quality, scale, and condition.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many images can I upload per artwork?">
    Up to 5 images per artwork.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What image formats or sizes are supported?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. Use standard image formats like JPEG or PNG, and check the upload form for any specific requirements shown at the time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I upload artwork that's already sold elsewhere?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. As a general practice on art marketplaces, only list work that is currently available for sale to avoid order conflicts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I save a listing as a draft before publishing?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. Check your dashboard for a save or draft option when creating a new listing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I fix a mistake after publishing?">
    See [Updating Artwork](/artist-guide/updating-artwork) for how to edit an existing listing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
