---
title: "Updating Artwork"
description: "Learn how to edit an existing artwork listing on Annapurna Art, including changing images, details, and price."
---

Annapurna Art lets artists edit and update artwork listings after they've been published. This guide covers how to make changes to a piece you've already listed.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact edit screens, field names, and any restrictions on what can be changed after a listing is live have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Sign in and go to your [Artist Dashboard](/artist-guide/artist-dashboard).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the listing you want to update">
    Go to your artwork section and locate the piece you want to edit. See [Managing Artwork](/artist-guide/managing-artwork) for how listings are organized.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the edit option">
    Select the listing and look for an edit or update option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your changes">
    Update the images, title, description, dimensions, or any other detail that needs correcting. You can share up to 5 images per artwork, so replace or reorder them as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the price (if needed)">
    Adjust the price if it needs to change. See [Artwork Pricing](/artist-guide/artwork-pricing) for what's known about pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Submit the update so the listing reflects your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/artist-guide/updating-artwork.png" alt="Edit form for updating an existing artwork listing" />
</Frame>

<Note>
Update your listings whenever a piece's availability, price, or details change. Keeping information accurate builds trust with collectors.
</Note>

<Warning>
It's not confirmed whether there are any restrictions on editing a listing once it has an active or pending order attached to it. If a piece is mid-transaction, check your dashboard or [contact support](/support/contact-support) before making changes.
</Warning>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit a listing after it has sold?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. Once a piece is marked sold, editing options may be limited. Check your dashboard directly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will collectors who saved my artwork to their wishlist see my updates?">
    This hasn't been confirmed, but typically wishlist entries reflect the current state of the listing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the price after someone has expressed interest?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. If an order is already pending, check with [support](/support/contact-support) before changing the price.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I want to remove the artwork entirely instead of editing it?">
    See [Managing Artwork](/artist-guide/managing-artwork) for options related to your overall listings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
