When a collector buys your artwork, Annapurna Art walks you through confirming the order, tracking delivery, and receiving payment. This page covers how that process works.

<Warning>
The overall order and payout flow described below is confirmed. However, the exact dashboard screens, labels, and notification details have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## How the order flow works

<Steps>
  <Step title="A collector places an order">
    A collector purchases one of your artwork listings through checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You see the order on your dashboard">
    The new order appears on your [Artist Dashboard](/artist-guide/artist-dashboard), where you can track and manage it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You confirm the order">
    You confirm the order from your dashboard to complete the transaction on your end.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The artwork is shipped and delivered">
    The order moves through fulfillment. Annapurna Art updates the delivery status on your dashboard once the order has been delivered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your payment is released">
    Once the delivery status is updated to show the order was delivered, your payment for the sale is released to you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/artist-guide/orders-and-sales.png" alt="Dashboard view of an order with delivery status and payout information" />
</Frame>

<Note>
Payout timing is tied to confirmed delivery, not to the moment of sale. Your payment is released after Annapurna Art marks the order as delivered on your dashboard — not immediately when the collector checks out.
</Note>

## Payout methods

Artists can receive payouts through:

- **Khalti** — a Nepali digital wallet
- **Credit Card**

<Warning>
How you select or set up your preferred payout method, and whether there are any payout minimums or schedules, have not been confirmed. Check your dashboard's payment or payout settings directly.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Confirm orders promptly from your dashboard so collectors aren't left waiting, and keep the delivery moving so your payout isn't delayed.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When do I get paid after a sale?">
    You're paid once Annapurna Art updates the delivery status on your dashboard to show the order has been delivered — not immediately at the time of sale.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payout methods are available?">
    Khalti (a Nepali digital wallet) or credit card.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does confirming an order actually do?">
    Confirming an order completes the transaction from the artist's side. The exact system behavior behind this action hasn't been independently verified.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who updates the delivery status — me or Annapurna Art?">
    Annapurna Art updates the delivery status on your dashboard once the order is delivered.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a collector's order is delayed or has an issue?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. See [Contact Support](/support/contact-support) if you run into an order issue you can't resolve from your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
