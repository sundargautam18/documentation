Once you're registered as an artist, Annapurna Art gives you access to a Supplier Dashboard for managing your portfolio and responding to sales — from anywhere in the world.

<Warning>
The core purpose of the Supplier Dashboard (portfolio management, order tracking, delivery status, payouts) is confirmed, but the exact widgets, layout, and labels shown on screen have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review). Confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## What you can do from the dashboard

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Manage your portfolio" icon="images">
    Add, edit, and update your artwork listings. See [Managing Artwork](/artist-guide/managing-artwork) and [Updating Artwork](/artist-guide/updating-artwork).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track and confirm orders" icon="box">
    View incoming orders and confirm them to complete the transaction. See [Orders & Sales](/artist-guide/orders-and-sales).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Monitor delivery status" icon="truck">
    Check delivery status as Annapurna Art updates it once an order ships and arrives.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track payouts" icon="wallet">
    See when payment is released to you — this happens once delivery status is updated as complete.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Accessing the dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your artist account">
    Sign in with the credentials you used during [Artist Registration](/artist-guide/artist-registration).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Supplier Dashboard">
    Look for a dashboard link in your account menu. This is your central hub for managing your presence on Annapurna Art.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your sections">
    Typical dashboard sections include your artwork listings, current orders, delivery statuses, and payout information. The exact section names and layout haven't been confirmed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Because the dashboard is accessible "from anywhere in the world," you should be able to manage your portfolio and respond to sales without needing to be at a specific location or device — though the exact platform support (desktop only vs. mobile-friendly) hasn't been confirmed.
</Note>

<Tip>
Check your dashboard regularly for new orders. Confirming orders promptly and keeping your portfolio up to date helps you respond to collectors quickly.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the Supplier Dashboard?">
    It's the dashboard Annapurna Art gives artists to manage their portfolio and respond to sales. It's the main tool you'll use after registering as an artist.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I access the dashboard on mobile?">
    This hasn't been confirmed. Annapurna Art states the dashboard works "from anywhere in the world," which suggests broad accessibility, but specific device or browser support isn't verified.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When do I get paid for a sale?">
    Payment is released once Annapurna Art updates the delivery status on your dashboard to indicate the order has been delivered. See [Orders & Sales](/artist-guide/orders-and-sales) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I add new artwork?">
    From the dashboard, look for an option to add or upload new artwork. See [Uploading Artwork](/artist-guide/uploading-artwork) for a step-by-step guide.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
