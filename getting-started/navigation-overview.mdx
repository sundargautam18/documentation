## Cart and wishlist

Most marketplaces place a cart or bag icon and a wishlist icon somewhere prominent in the header, often near the account menu. On Annapurna Art, you can generally expect similar shortcuts, though their exact placement and icons have not been confirmed.

<Tip>
If you find artwork you like but aren't ready to buy, look for a wishlist option (often a heart icon) to save it for later. See [Adding to Wishlist](/buyer-guide/adding-to-wishlist) for more.
</Tip>

## Footer and support links

Marketplace sites typically also include a footer with links to company information, policies, and support. On Annapurna Art, this is a reasonable place to look for a [contact page](https://annapurnaart.com/contact-us) if you need help.

<Note>
Exact menu labels, icon placement, and footer contents haven't been independently verified for Annapurna Art. Treat the names used on this page (like "account menu" or "wishlist icon") as general descriptions rather than confirmed labels.
</Note>
