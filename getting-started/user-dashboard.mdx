After you log in to Annapurna Art, you land in your personal account area. This page gives you a general overview of what's typically there and where to go for more detail on each section.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, field names, and labels have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## Collector (buyer) dashboard

As a collector, your dashboard is generally the hub for managing your shopping activity and account details. As with most marketplaces of this kind, you can typically expect to find:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Order History" icon="receipt" href="/account-management/order-history">
    Review past and current orders, including status and details.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wishlist" icon="heart" href="/account-management/wishlist">
    Revisit artwork you've saved for later.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Profile Settings" icon="user-gear" href="/account-management/profile-settings">
    Update your name, contact details, and preferences.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notifications" icon="bell" href="/account-management/notifications">
    Manage alerts about orders, price changes, or artist updates.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
If you can't find a setting mentioned here, it may be labeled differently on the live site, or located under your account menu rather than a dedicated dashboard page. Refer to [Navigation Overview](/getting-started/navigation-overview) for general guidance on where things live.
</Tip>

## Artist dashboard: a different experience

<Info>
If you sell artwork on Annapurna Art, you use a separate **Supplier Dashboard** rather than the collector dashboard described above. The Supplier Dashboard is generally geared toward managing your portfolio, responding to sales, and tracking orders as a seller. See [Artist Dashboard](/artist-guide/artist-dashboard) for details.
</Info>

If you're both a collector and an artist, you may need to switch between these two views, depending on how Annapurna Art structures artist accounts. This hasn't been confirmed, so check the live site for how account switching works if you hold both roles.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I go to change my password or email?">
    Password and profile changes are typically found under your profile settings. See [Profile Settings](/account-management/profile-settings) and [Password Management](/account-management/password-management).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I see the status of an order from my dashboard?">
    Yes, order status is generally available from your order history. See [Order History](/account-management/order-history) and [Order Tracking](/buyer-guide/order-tracking).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get access to the Supplier Dashboard?">
    The Supplier Dashboard is generally available once you've signed up and been approved as an artist. See [Becoming an Artist](/artist-guide/becoming-an-artist) to get started.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
