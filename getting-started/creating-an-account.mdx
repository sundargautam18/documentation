Annapurna Art is an online marketplace where you can discover and buy original paintings, sculptures, photography, and drawings directly from artists around the world. Creating an account lets you save favorite pieces, track orders, and complete purchases.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, field names, and labels have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

This guide walks you through creating a collector (buyer) account. If you're an artist who wants to sell your work on Annapurna Art, see [Becoming an Artist](/artist-guide/becoming-an-artist) instead — artist registration follows a separate path with additional steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
    From the [Annapurna Art homepage](https://annapurnaart.com/), open your account menu and choose the option to sign up or create an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the collector/buyer option">
    If the sign-up flow asks you to choose a role, select the option for buying or collecting artwork rather than selling it. This keeps your account on the collector path rather than the artist path.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the sign-up form">
    Enter the details requested, typically your name, email address, and a password. Some marketplaces also offer sign-up through a third-party account (like Google), if available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and accept the terms">
    Read the terms of service and privacy policy, then confirm that you agree to them before submitting the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Submit your details to create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email (if required)">
    Check your inbox for a confirmation message and follow the link or instructions provided to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Many marketplaces require you to verify your email address before you can complete a purchase. This step has not been confirmed for Annapurna Art specifically, but it's a common practice — check your inbox (including your spam folder) after signing up.
</Note>

<Tip>
Use an email address you check regularly. Order confirmations, shipping updates, and password reset links are typically sent there.
</Tip>

<Tip>
Choose a strong, unique password. If you ever need to change it, see [Password Management](/account-management/password-management).
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is creating a collector account free?">
    Signing up to browse and buy artwork is generally free on marketplaces like this one. You typically only pay when you complete a purchase.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch my account to an artist account later?">
    Many marketplaces allow existing users to apply to sell as well as buy. If Annapurna Art supports this, you'd typically find the option to sign up as an artist from your account menu or a dedicated artist page. See [Becoming an Artist](/artist-guide/becoming-an-artist) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive a verification email. What should I do?">
    Check your spam or junk folder first. If you still don't see it, look for an option to resend the verification email, or see [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) for further help.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need an account to browse artwork?">
    Marketplaces like this one usually let you browse categories and artist profiles without an account, but you'll need one to save items to a wishlist or complete a purchase.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
