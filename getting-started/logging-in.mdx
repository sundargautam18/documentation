Once you have an Annapurna Art account, logging in gives you access to your wishlist, order history, and account settings.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, field names, and labels have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

If you don't have an account yet, see [Creating an Account](/getting-started/creating-an-account) for collectors or [Becoming an Artist](/artist-guide/becoming-an-artist) if you plan to sell artwork.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    From the [Annapurna Art homepage](https://annapurnaart.com/), open your account menu and choose the option to log in or sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter the email address and password you used to create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the login form">
    Confirm your details to sign in. You should be taken to your dashboard or back to the page you were browsing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  <img src="/images/getting-started/logging-in.png" alt="Login form for signing in to an Annapurna Art account" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
If your browser offers to save your password, this can make future logins faster. Avoid saving passwords on shared or public computers.
</Tip>

## Resetting your password

If you've forgotten your password, look for a "forgot password" link near the login form. For the full reset process, see [Password Management](/account-management/password-management).

## Troubleshooting login issues

<Note>
If you're having trouble logging in — for example, your credentials aren't being accepted, or you're not receiving a password reset email — see [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) for common fixes. If the issue persists, [contact support](/support/contact-support).
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm sure my password is correct, but I still can't log in">
    Double-check that you're using the same email address you signed up with, and that Caps Lock isn't on. If the problem continues, try resetting your password via [Password Management](/account-management/password-management).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I stay logged in between visits?">
    Many sites offer a "remember me" option at login that keeps you signed in on that browser. Availability on Annapurna Art hasn't been confirmed — look for this option on the login form.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I no longer have access to my account email?">
    Contact [support](/support/contact-support) for help recovering your account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
