## What a featured collection typically is

On many art marketplaces, a featured or trending collection is a grouping of artwork that the platform surfaces for extra visibility, separate from a themed virtual exhibition. Common ways marketplaces build these groupings include:

- **Trending or popular artwork** — pieces getting more views, saves, or recent sales.
- **Staff picks or featured artwork** — pieces manually selected by the platform's team.
- **New arrivals** — recently listed pieces from artists across the marketplace.

<Note>
A related, indirect signal (a third-party beta site referencing a "trending arts" page) suggests Annapurna Art likely has some form of featured or trending grouping, but this has not been confirmed directly on Annapurna Art. Treat the categories listed above as illustrative examples of how such collections often work elsewhere, not a confirmed list of what exists on Annapurna Art today.
</Note>

## How this differs from a virtual exhibition

A virtual exhibition is typically organized around a specific theme, medium, or artist spotlight and may include added context, such as an introduction or curator notes. A featured collection is usually more lightweight — a rotating list of artwork surfaced for visibility rather than a themed narrative. The exact distinction (or whether Annapurna Art treats them as separate features at all) has not been confirmed.
