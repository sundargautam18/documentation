Keeping your password up to date helps protect your Annapurna Art account. This page covers the typical steps for changing your password while logged in, and for resetting it if you've forgotten it.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, field names, and options have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## Changing your password

If you're already logged in and just want to update your password, this is typically done from your profile or account settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your account settings">
    Log in and go to your profile or account settings. See [Profile Settings](/account-management/profile-settings) for general guidance on where account options tend to live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the password option">
    Look for a section related to security or password, often labeled something like "Change Password."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current and new password">
    You'll typically need to confirm your current password before setting a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Confirm the update. You may be logged out of other sessions or asked to log in again after changing your password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resetting a forgotten password

If you can't log in because you've forgotten your password, use the password reset flow from the login page instead.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Open the [login page](/getting-started/logging-in) and look for a "forgot password" link near the login form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your account email">
    Provide the email address associated with your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email for a reset link">
    Annapurna Art should send an email with a link or code to reset your password. Check your spam or junk folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a new password">
    Follow the link to create a new password, then log in with your new credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Choose a password you haven't used elsewhere, and consider using a password manager to keep track of it securely.
</Tip>

<Note>
If you don't receive a reset email, or the reset link doesn't work, see [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) or [contact support](/support/contact-support) for help.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How often should I change my password?">
    There's no confirmed requirement from Annapurna Art. As a general practice, it's a good idea to update your password periodically or immediately if you suspect it's been compromised.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I don't receive the password reset email?">
    Check your spam or junk folder first. If it still doesn't arrive, see [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) or [contact support](/support/contact-support).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will changing my password log me out of other devices?">
    This is common practice for many sites as a security measure, but it hasn't been confirmed for Annapurna Art. Be prepared to log in again on your other devices after a password change.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
