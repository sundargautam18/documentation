## Typical notification types

A marketplace like Annapurna Art typically includes notifications such as:

- **Order updates** — confirmation, shipping, and delivery status changes for purchases you've made.
- **Sale alerts** — for artists, notifications when a piece sells or a buyer places an order.
- **Wishlist price drops** — alerts when a saved item's price changes.
- **Account and security notices** — such as password changes or login from a new device.

<Note>
This list is illustrative, not a confirmed feature set. Annapurna Art may offer some, all, or none of these notification types, and may use different names or groupings. Check your account settings on the live site for what's actually available.
</Note>

## Managing your notification preferences

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your account settings">
    Log in and go to your profile or account settings. See [Profile Settings](/account-management/profile-settings) for general guidance on where account options tend to live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the notifications section">
    Look for a section labeled something like "Notifications" or "Preferences."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the available options">
    You may be able to turn specific notification types on or off, and choose how you receive them, such as by email or in-app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Confirm your updated preferences if a save step is required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you're missing order or sale notifications, check your spam or junk folder before assuming they weren't sent, and confirm your email address in [Profile Settings](/account-management/profile-settings) is correct and verified.
</Tip>
