Checkout is where you finalize a purchase on Annapurna Art: confirming what you're buying, where it should ship, and how you'll pay for it. This page walks through the flow you can typically expect.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, payment options, and timeframes have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## Checkout steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your order">
    Before proceeding, confirm you're purchasing the correct piece, at the correct price. Some marketplaces show a cart or order summary at this stage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in or create an account">
    If you're not already signed in, you'll typically be asked to log in or create an account before you can complete a purchase. See [Creating an Account](/getting-started/creating-an-account) or [Logging In](/getting-started/logging-in).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter shipping details">
    Provide the address where the artwork should be delivered. Since shipping is paid by the collector on Annapurna Art, this step may also show an estimated or calculated shipping cost. See [Shipping Information](/buyer-guide/shipping-information).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a payment method">
    Select from the payment options presented at checkout and enter the required details, such as your saved payment method. See [Payment Methods](/buyer-guide/payment-methods) for what's currently confirmed about buyer-facing payment options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the total and confirm">
    Double-check the item price, any shipping cost, and the total before submitting your order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive confirmation">
    After your order is placed, you should see an on-screen confirmation and typically receive a confirmation email with your order details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Double-check your shipping address before confirming your order. Original artwork can be delicate and costly to reroute or reship if delivered to the wrong address.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods can I use at checkout?">
    This hasn't been independently verified. See [Payment Methods](/buyer-guide/payment-methods) for what's confirmed versus unverified, and check the payment options shown at checkout on the live site.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is shipping cost included in the item price?">
    No. Shipping is paid by the collector separately from the artwork price. See [Shipping Information](/buyer-guide/shipping-information) for details.
  </Accordion>
