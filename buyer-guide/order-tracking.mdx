After you purchase a piece, you'll likely want to know when it ships and when it will arrive. This page covers what's known about order tracking on Annapurna Art as a buyer.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, payment options, and timeframes have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## What's confirmed

Annapurna Art's artist-facing materials describe artists updating delivery status on a dashboard once a piece has shipped or been delivered. This confirms that order status information exists on the platform in some form.

<Info>
  What's confirmed is that delivery status gets updated on the artist side (through the Supplier Dashboard) once an order ships or is delivered. Whether collectors see an equivalent order-tracking screen has not been directly confirmed, though it's a reasonable and typical feature for a marketplace like this to offer.
</Info>

## What a buyer-facing tracking experience might look like

<Warning>
  The following is a generic, hedged description based on common marketplace patterns. It has not been verified against the live Annapurna Art product.
</Warning>

As with most online marketplaces, you might expect to find order status information in a couple of places:

- **Order confirmation email** — sent when you complete a purchase, and possibly updated or followed up when your order ships
- **Order history in your account** — a list of past and current orders, potentially showing a status such as processing, shipped, or delivered
