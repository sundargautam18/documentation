Shipping matters more for original artwork than for many other online purchases, since pieces can be fragile, large, or otherwise require careful handling. This page covers what's confirmed about shipping on Annapurna Art and hedges everything that isn't.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, payment options, and timeframes have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

## Who pays for shipping

<Info>
  Shipping cost on Annapurna Art is paid by the collector, not the artist. This is confirmed.
</Info>

This means the price you see on an artwork's detail page typically reflects the piece itself, and shipping is calculated or added separately, most likely during checkout.

## How shipping is handled

Annapurna Art's artist-facing marketing describes shipping as being "handled by specialists." This suggests the platform works with dedicated shipping partners for moving artwork, rather than leaving logistics entirely to individual artists, but the exact process hasn't been independently verified.

<Note>
  What "handled by specialists" means in practice, such as which carriers are used, how packaging is managed, or whether this applies to every order, hasn't been confirmed. Treat this as a general assurance rather than a documented process.
</Note>

## What isn't confirmed

<Warning>
  The following details are not verified for Annapurna Art. Do not treat any specific numbers below as fact — they are not provided because they haven't been confirmed.
</Warning>

- **Shipping cost calculation** — whether it's a flat rate, based on size or weight, or based on destination
- **Delivery timeframes** — how long shipping typically takes domestically or internationally
- **International shipping** — whether all pieces can ship worldwide, or whether some are restricted by size, fragility, or destination
- **Carriers or shipping partners** — which companies are used to move artwork

For any of these, check the shipping details shown at checkout for your specific order, since they likely vary by piece, size, and destination.

## Typical shipping considerations for art marketplaces

As with most art marketplaces, a few factors commonly affect shipping cost and timeframe, even though the specifics aren't confirmed for Annapurna Art.

| Factor | Why it typically matters |
|---|---|
| Size and weight of the piece | Larger or heavier pieces usually cost more to ship and may need special packaging |
| Fragility | Paintings, sculptures, and framed work often require protective crating or packaging |
| Destination | International shipments typically take longer and cost more than domestic ones |
| Insurance | Valuable original artwork may be insured during transit |
