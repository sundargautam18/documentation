Payment on Annapurna Art works differently depending on whether you're paying for a purchase as a collector or receiving payout as an artist. This page separates what's confirmed from what's still unverified for each side of the marketplace.

<Warning>
This page describes the typical flow for a marketplace like Annapurna Art. The exact steps, screens, payment options, and timeframes have not been independently verified against the live product (the site blocks automated review) — confirm against the live site and update this page before publishing.
</Warning>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Collectors">
    <Warning>
      Buyer-facing payment methods have not been independently verified for Annapurna Art. The information below is generic and hedged — check the payment options shown at checkout on the live site for what's currently available.
    </Warning>

    As with most art marketplaces, checkout likely presents a set of payment options for you to choose from, such as a card or a saved payment method. However, the specific payment gateways or providers Annapurna Art supports for collectors have not been confirmed.

    <Info>
      Do not assume any specific payment provider is available until you see it listed at checkout. Refer to the options shown on the live checkout page rather than this documentation for the current list.
    </Info>

    ### What to expect at checkout

    - A choice between one or more payment options
    - A form to enter or select your payment details
    - A summary of the item price, any shipping cost, and the total before you confirm

    For the full checkout flow, see [Checkout Process](/buyer-guide/checkout-process).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Artists">
    Artist payouts on Annapurna Art are confirmed to support the following methods:

    - **Khalti**
    - **Credit Card**

    <Info>
      These are the confirmed payout methods for artists receiving payment for sold artwork. This is separate from how collectors pay at checkout, which is not confirmed (see the "For Collectors" tab).
    </Info>

    For more on managing your payouts as an artist, see [Orders and Sales](/artist-guide/orders-and-sales).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
