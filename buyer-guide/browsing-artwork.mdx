Annapurna Art organizes its marketplace into categories and themed collections so you can explore original artwork without needing to search for something specific. This page covers the main ways you can browse as a collector.

## Browse by category

Annapurna Art groups artwork into categories such as painting, sculpture, photography, and drawing. You can view all artwork across every category, or narrow your view to a single category at a time.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/buyer-guide/browsing-artwork.png" alt="Category browsing page on Annapurna Art showing artwork thumbnails" />
</Frame>

<Info>
  You can view all artwork on the marketplace at once, or drill into a specific category like painting. Category pages typically show a grid of artwork thumbnails with the artist name, title, and price.
</Info>

## Browse by theme or country

In addition to category, Annapurna Art lets you explore artwork grouped by theme, such as artwork associated with a particular country or region. This is a useful way to discover pieces connected to a place or style you're interested in, rather than a specific medium.

<Note>
  The exact list of themes and countries available for browsing hasn't been independently verified. What's confirmed is that theme-based and country-based browsing exists alongside category browsing — check the live site for the current set of options.
</Note>

## Browse by artist

Every artist on Annapurna Art has a profile page that lists the work they have for sale. If you find a piece you like, visiting the artist's profile is a good way to see their full body of work and discover similar pieces.

<Tip>
  If you want to look at a specific piece more closely before deciding, see [Viewing Artwork Details](/buyer-guide/viewing-artwork-details).
</Tip>

## Combining browsing with search and filters

Browsing works well when you want to explore broadly, but if you already have something specific in mind, such as a medium, price range, or keyword, searching and filtering will usually get you there faster.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need an account to browse artwork?">
    Marketplaces like this one typically let you browse categories and artist profiles without signing in. You'll usually need an account to save items to a wishlist or complete a purchase.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I browse by price range?">
    Category pages support some form of filtering, but the exact filter options haven't been independently verified. See [Searching & Filtering](/buyer-guide/searching-filtering) for what's confirmed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I see more of an artist's work?">
    Open the artist's profile from any of their artwork pages to see their full portfolio of available pieces.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
