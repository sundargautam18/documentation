If you've worked through the [FAQ](/support/faq) and [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) pages and still need help, the best way to reach the Annapurna Art team is through their official Contact Us page.

<Warning>
Support channels, response times, and some answers below are not fully documented publicly. Where a specific detail isn't confirmed, this page says so explicitly rather than guessing — verify with the Annapurna Art team before publishing.
</Warning>

## Get in touch

<Card title="Contact Us" icon="headset" href="https://annapurnaart.com/contact-us">
  Visit the official Annapurna Art Contact Us page to reach the team.
</Card>

<Note>
Support hours, response-time expectations, and whether email, phone, or live chat are available aren't published anywhere we could confirm. Check the [Contact Us](https://annapurnaart.com/contact-us) page directly for the current options and any stated response times.
</Note>

## Before you reach out

<Tip>
Having a few details ready can help the team resolve your question faster, even though the exact information they'll ask for isn't documented.
</Tip>

Consider having the following on hand when you contact the team:

- The email address associated with your Annapurna Art account
- Whether you're contacting them as a buyer or an artist
- Any relevant order number, artwork title, or screenshot related to your issue
- Steps you've already tried, for example from the [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) page
