This page collects answers to common questions about buying and selling on Annapurna Art. It only covers questions where the answer has been confirmed — for anything else, reach out to the team directly.

<Warning>
Support channels, response times, and some answers below are not fully documented publicly. Where a specific detail isn't confirmed, this page says so explicitly rather than guessing — verify with the Annapurna Art team before publishing.
</Warning>

<Info>
Can't find your question here? This list only includes questions with a confirmed answer. For anything else, see [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting) or [Contact Support](/support/contact-support).
</Info>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Buyers">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Who pays for shipping?">
        The collector pays for shipping. Shipping costs are not covered by the artist or by Annapurna Art. See [Shipping Information](/buyer-guide/shipping-information) for more on how shipping works.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="What payment methods can I use as a buyer?">
        This isn't publicly documented yet. See [Payment Methods](/buyer-guide/payment-methods) for what's currently known, and [contact support](/support/contact-support) for the current answer if you need confirmation before purchasing.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Can I return artwork after purchase?">
        Annapurna Art's return policy isn't publicly documented yet. [Contact support](/support/contact-support) for the current answer before you buy if this affects your decision.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Does Annapurna Art ship internationally?">
        This isn't publicly documented yet. See [Shipping Information](/buyer-guide/shipping-information) for what's currently known, and [contact support](/support/contact-support) to confirm for your location.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Artists">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Is it free to sign up as an artist?">
        Yes. Signing up as an artist on Annapurna Art is free. See [Becoming an Artist](/artist-guide/becoming-an-artist) and [Artist Registration](/artist-guide/artist-registration) to get started.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do artists get paid?">
        Artists get paid once the delivery status of an order is confirmed on their dashboard. See [Orders & Sales](/artist-guide/orders-and-sales) for more on how orders and payouts work.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="What payment methods can artists use to get paid?">
        Artists can receive payouts via Khalti or credit card. See [Orders & Sales](/artist-guide/orders-and-sales) for details on the payout process.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Who pays for shipping when I sell a piece?">
        The collector pays for shipping, not the artist. See [Orders & Sales](/artist-guide/orders-and-sales) for how this fits into the order flow.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Does Annapurna Art take a commission on sales?">
        This isn't publicly documented yet. See [Artwork Pricing](/artist-guide/artwork-pricing) for what's currently known, and [contact support](/support/contact-support) for the current answer.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
