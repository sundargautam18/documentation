title: "Troubleshooting"
description: "Generic troubleshooting steps for common Annapurna Art issues, including login, artwork uploads, checkout, and order status."
---

If something isn't working as expected on Annapurna Art, work through the relevant flow below before reaching out to the team. These steps are intentionally general — they cover the kinds of checks that resolve most common issues on any marketplace platform.

<Warning>
Support channels, response times, and some answers below are not fully documented publicly. Where a specific detail isn't confirmed, this page says so explicitly rather than guessing — verify with the Annapurna Art team before publishing.
</Warning>

<Info>
None of the specific error messages or bug behaviors below have been independently verified against the live product. If your issue doesn't match these general patterns, skip ahead to [Contact Support](/support/contact-support).
</Info>

## I can't log in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Double-check your credentials">
    Confirm you're using the email address you originally signed up with, and that Caps Lock isn't affecting your password entry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    If you're not sure your password is correct, use the "forgot password" option on the login page. See [Password Management](/account-management/password-management) for the reset process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email access">
    Make sure you still have access to the email address on your account, since password resets and account notifications go there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Still stuck?">
    If you've tried the above and still can't log in, [contact support](/support/contact-support).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Artwork upload is failing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the upload requirements">
    Check that your image files meet the format and size expectations described in [Uploading Artwork](/artist-guide/uploading-artwork).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a smaller or different file">
    Large image files or unsupported formats are a common cause of upload failures on most platforms. Try compressing the image or saving it in a different common format (such as JPG or PNG).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your connection and retry">
    A slow or interrupted connection can cause an upload to fail partway through. Refresh the page and try again on a stable connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Still stuck?">
    If uploads keep failing after these checks, [contact support](/support/contact-support) with details on the artwork you're trying to upload.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment or checkout issue

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your order details">
    Confirm the artwork, quantity, and shipping details are correct before submitting payment. See [Checkout Process](/buyer-guide/checkout-process) for what to expect at each step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your payment details">
    Double-check the payment information you entered, and confirm your payment method has sufficient funds or available credit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try again or use a different payment method">
    If a payment attempt fails, try resubmitting once, or use an alternate payment method if one is available to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Still stuck?">
    If a payment issue persists or you're charged without a confirmed order, [contact support](/support/contact-support) as soon as possible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Order status isn't updating

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="As a buyer">
    Check [Order Tracking](/buyer-guide/order-tracking) and [Order History](/account-management/order-history) for the latest status. Order and shipping updates can take time to reflect, especially soon after a purchase. If the status seems stuck for an extended period, [contact support](/support/contact-support).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="As an artist">
    Check [Orders & Sales](/artist-guide/orders-and-sales) on your dashboard for the current delivery status. Since payout is tied to confirmed delivery status, an order that isn't marked as delivered will also affect when you get paid. If a status seems incorrect or stuck, [contact support](/support/contact-support).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
